Building a Travel Blog Page
  • Imagine that you are a passionate traveler who wants to share your adventures with the world through a travel
    blog. You've decided to create a web page to showcase your recent trip to an exotic destination. Your web page will
    serve as a post about this trip, and you want it to be engaging and visually appealing. You'll use HTML to create the
    content for your blog post.
  • Details:
    1. Destination: You can choose any exotic destination you like (e.g., a tropical island, a historic city, a wildlife safari, etc.).
    2. Introduction: In the introduction paragraph, provide a brief overview of the destination. Explain why you chose to visit this place and what makes it special.
    3. Ordered List: Create an ordered list of at least five must-visit places or activities in the destination. Include a short description with each item to give readers a glimpse of what they can expect.
